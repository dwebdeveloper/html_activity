Barcelona, Spain

Top 3 Destination Attractions to Visit!

Barcelona, a city full of history, culture, and unforgetable sights, is one place nobody will not regret visiting. As for me, I'll be booking my trip with Tripadvivsor!

Camp Nou

  • Home stadium of Barcelona
  • Largest stadium in Spain
  • Seating capacity of 99,354

Palau de la Música Catalana

  • Only concert venue in this style to be listed as a World Heritage Site by UNESCO
  • The Palau de la Música Catalana is an architectural jewel of Catalan Art Nouveau
  • built between 1905 and 1908 by the modernist architect Lluís Domènech i Montaner as a home for the Orfeó Català

Teatre Grec

  • Home to Barcelona's summertime outdoor cultural event, the Festival Grec, since 1976.
  • Built for the 1929 Barcelona International Exposition
  • Open-air theatre in the Montjuïc hill in Barcelona, Catalonia, Spain. Inspired by the Greek Epidaurus theatre, the Grec was designed by architects Ramon Reventós and Nicolau Maria Rubió i Tudurí
